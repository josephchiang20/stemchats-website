STEMlights

STEMlights is a weekly newsletter that aims to explore the intersectionality of STEM through journalism! Our newsletter highlights various STEM insights, professionals, and opportunities, while also shining light on how science can be applied, developed, and learned.

STEMlights provides a knowledgeable-youth perspective on important and topical STEM issues. Our team aspires to create an environment for students, by students that encourages curiosity and creative liberty, working to provide more educational support for underrepresented students.

Impact

As a newsletter by students, for students, our goal is to explore the intersectionality of STEM sentiments in a creative manner. All team members within STEMlights work to encourage curiosity and promote educational equity by creating weekly content for our audience. Our newsletter covers a range of topics: STEM breakthroughs, opportunities, youth leaders, professionals, pop culture, politics, history, etc. By diving into the multiple applications of STEM, we hope to demonstrate that science can be enjoyed by anyone!

As of March 2021, our newsletter serves nearly 700 subscribers with an average 38.7% open rate. Our team has worked hard to release almost 50 newsletters that are opened by high school and college students from all over the world.

For full editions, visit our website!
